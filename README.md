# AutoLatch

Doors should feel smart, not fussy.

AutoLatch gives Minecraft doors a clean, server-side auto-close behavior that feels like it was always meant to be there. Open a wooden door, walk through, and let the world tidy itself behind you. No chat spam, no particles, no commands, no client-side gimmicks. Just quiet, reliable door behavior for survival worlds, servers, builds, and modpacks that want polish without noise.

AutoLatch is built for players who love immersion and server owners who love predictable mechanics. Oak doors, spruce doors, and other eligible wooden doors close themselves after a configurable delay, while iron doors and redstone-controlled doors stay protected by default. Blacklists, whitelists, modded-door support, redstone respect, and capped retry behavior give pack makers the control they need without turning a small convenience mod into a maintenance chore.

It is lightweight, server-authoritative, and deliberately invisible until it helps. The door closes, the vanilla sound plays, nearby players see the update, and the world simply feels a little more finished.

AutoLatch: open the door, keep moving.
